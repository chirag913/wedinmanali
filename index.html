Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your Manali destination wedding.

What is the best time to have a wedding in Manali?

The peak seasons are April-June and September-November with pleasant weather. December-February offers magical snow weddings. July-August is monsoon season with occasional rain but lush green landscapes.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking at least 6-8 months in advance, especially for peak season dates. For winter weddings during December-January, booking 8-10 months ahead is advisable.

Can you accommodate international guests?

Absolutely! We provide complete assistance for international guests including airport transfers, accommodation arrangements, and local guidance. We can also arrange special cultural experiences for them.

What makes your 8 lakh package affordable?

Our local connections, insider knowledge of hidden venues, and direct relationships with vendors allow us to eliminate middlemen costs while maintaining quality. We also optimize resources without compromising on experience.

How do we reach Manali?

The nearest airport is Bhuntar Airport (Kullu-Manali Airport) about 50km away. For better connectivity, Chandigarh Airport (295km) is recommended. We arrange comfortable transportation from these locations to Manali.

Do you handle legal paperwork for weddings?

Yes, we provide guidance and assistance with all legal requirements for both Indian and foreign nationals. For court marriages, we can arrange the entire process with local authorities.
